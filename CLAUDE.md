# Pizza Yolo Website Documentation

## Purpose

This repository contains the complete documentation for designing and developing the official Pizza Yolo website.

Before reading any other document, carefully understand the philosophy of the project.

This is not just another restaurant website.

The objective is to create one of the strongest food brands in Hungary through exceptional branding, thoughtful UX, high-quality design, excellent performance and memorable storytelling.

Every design decision should support the brand.

Every interaction should feel intentional.

Every section should have a purpose.

Never add elements simply because restaurant websites usually have them.

Question every decision.

---

# About Pizza Yolo

Pizza Yolo is an American-inspired Detroit Style Pizza concept located on the beach of Club Aliga, Balatonaliga, Hungary.

The concept combines:

- Detroit Style Pizza
- Beach culture
- Summer lifestyle
- Community
- Streetwear-inspired branding
- Modern digital marketing

The business operates as a beach fast-food concept focused on serving Detroit Style Pizza slices.

The menu is intentionally small.

Customers don't choose from dozens of products.

Instead, they choose quickly and spend more time enjoying the beach.

The simplicity is part of the experience.

---

# Brand Philosophy

We are not trying to become another pizza restaurant.

We are building a recognizable lifestyle brand around pizza.

People should remember:

- the atmosphere
- the branding
- the events
- the packaging
- the experience

as much as the food itself.

Pizza is the product.

The brand is the business.

---

# Design Philosophy

The design should feel confident.

Not loud.

Not childish.

Not overdesigned.

Not luxurious.

Not corporate.

Instead it should feel like a modern streetwear brand that happens to sell pizza.

The website should feel premium through simplicity.

White space is valuable.

Large typography is encouraged.

Photography should carry the experience.

Avoid visual clutter.

Every component should have a reason to exist.

---

# Development Philosophy

Always prioritize:

1. User experience
2. Performance
3. Accessibility
4. SEO
5. Visual consistency

Do not over-engineer.

Do not create unnecessary animations.

Do not add unnecessary libraries.

Keep everything modular.

Keep everything maintainable.

---

# User Experience Philosophy

Visitors should immediately understand:

What Pizza Yolo is.

Where it is.

Why it is different.

How to visit.

The website should remove friction.

The website should never feel complicated.

---

# Brand Emotion

The website should feel like discovering a place you immediately want to visit with friends this weekend.

Visitors should imagine themselves:

Eating pizza.

Watching the sunset.

Hanging out.

Listening to music.

Swimming.

Taking photos.

Relaxing.

The website sells a summer feeling first.

Pizza second.

---

# Working Method

This documentation is intentionally separated into multiple Markdown documents.

Each document describes one aspect of the project.

Always follow the documentation hierarchy.

If two documents appear to conflict, prioritize the document that is more specific to the topic.

Never invent new branding elements that are not documented.

Instead, build upon the existing design language.

---

# Creative Freedom

Creative ideas are encouraged.

However, every new idea should satisfy at least one of these conditions:

- improves usability
- improves conversion
- improves brand consistency
- improves storytelling
- improves SEO
- improves accessibility
- improves performance

Avoid adding features only because they look impressive.

Good design is invisible.

---

# Goal

The final website should become one of the strongest visual restaurant websites in Hungary while remaining fast, simple, memorable and easy to maintain.

Every decision should support that goal.
