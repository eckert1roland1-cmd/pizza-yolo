# Pizza Yolo SEO Strategy

---

# Purpose

This document defines the SEO strategy for Pizza Yolo.

The objective is not to rank for every pizza-related keyword in Hungary.

Instead, the goal is to dominate a very specific niche while building long-term organic authority around Detroit Style Pizza and Pizza Yolo as a brand.

The website should be built for people first.

SEO should naturally emerge from excellent content, fast performance and clear information architecture.

Never sacrifice user experience for search engine rankings.

---

# SEO Philosophy

Google increasingly rewards websites that provide:

• Helpful content

• Clear structure

• Strong brand signals

• Fast loading

• Mobile usability

• Genuine authority

Pizza Yolo should become the authority for Detroit Style Pizza in Hungary.

Not through keyword stuffing.

Through quality.

---

# Primary Business Goal

SEO exists to bring people to the physical location.

The goal is not pageviews.

The goal is visits.

Every page should ultimately answer:

"Why should I visit Pizza Yolo today?"

---

# Primary Search Intent

Our visitors are usually searching for one of these:

"I want pizza nearby."

"I want to eat at Balaton."

"I'm looking for a cool place."

"What is Detroit Style Pizza?"

"What should we eat today?"

The website should satisfy these intents naturally.

# Internal Linking

---

# Image SEO

Every image should include:

Descriptive filename

Example:

detroit-style-pizza-pepperoni-balaton.webp

Not:

IMG_4821.jpg

Every image needs meaningful alt text.

Describe what is actually visible.

Compress all images.

Prefer WebP.

Use responsive image sizes.

Lazy load below the fold.
