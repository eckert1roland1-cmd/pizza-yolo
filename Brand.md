# Pizza Yolo — Brand Guidelines

## Project Overview

Pizza Yolo is a Detroit Style Pizza concept located at Club Aliga, Balatonvilágos.

The brand is built around one simple idea:

Detroit Style Pizza by the beach.

The goal is not to become another beach buffet, but to become a recognizable summer destination where people naturally gather before, during and after spending time on the beach.

---

# Brand Vision

Create the most recognizable Detroit Style Pizza brand in Hungary while building a modern summer lifestyle brand around it.

The restaurant should feel more like discovering a cool streetwear brand than visiting a traditional restaurant.

---

# Brand Mission

Serve consistently high-quality Detroit Style Pizza while creating an atmosphere people want to come back to.

The experience should be simple.

Fast food.
High quality.
Relaxed atmosphere.
Good music.
Great branding.

# Brand Personality

Pizza Yolo should always feel

- Young
- Relaxed
- Modern
- Friendly
- Confident
- Clean
- Authentic
- Slightly playful

Never

- Cheap
- Loud
- Forced
- Corporate
- Fake American
- Overly luxurious

---

# Positioning

Pizza Yolo is NOT

- an Italian restaurant
- a gourmet restaurant
- a traditional beach buffet

Pizza Yolo IS

Detroit Style Pizza.

Served fast.

By the beach.

---

# Brand Values

Consistency

Every slice should taste identical.

Quality

Fresh ingredients.

Good recipes.

No shortcuts.

Community

People should remember the atmosphere as much as the pizza.

Accessibility

Simple menu.

Easy ordering.

No unnecessary complexity.

---

# Tone of Voice

Keep copy short.

Confident.

Natural.

Friendly.

Never oversell.

Avoid marketing buzzwords.

Examples

✔ Fresh Like You.

✔ Love at First Slice.

✔ See You by the Lake.

✘ Award Winning

✘ Premium Experience

✘ Authentic Handmade Craftsmanship

# Customer Experience

A customer should leave thinking

"That was much cooler than I expected."

rather than

"That pizza was okay."

The brand experience should be memorable before, during and after eating.

Brand recognition should come from consistency across every touchpoint:

- website
- packaging
- social media
- menu boards
- merchandise
- signage
- events

Everything should feel like it belongs to the same world.
